Target audience: Our target audience is anyone that wants to see the beauty of the Osa Peninsula of Costa Rica! National Geographic stated that it is the "most biologically intense place on earth." Therefore, we are targeting people who love nature and want to have an experience with plants, fish, animals, and other forms of life that they cannot experience anywhere else.

Personas 1:

25-35 year old Honeymooners. Looking for the most memerable honeymoon? Osa Vida is the best place to start! Whether you want to go on a sunset Tour or a deep sea fishing trip Osa Vida is just for you. On the Osa vida Website you can see everything that Osa Vida provides!

personas 2:

Young Families 2-6 kids. Feeling stuck up in your house and needing to get away? Osa Vida is perfect for a family get away! Your kids will love the snorkiling trip! The Snorkiling trip is the best! There is Food on the boat, paddleboards, a slide, and you can ask the driver to stop while the family takes turns jumping of the boat platform into the bright blue sea.
